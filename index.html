TSI Tutoring Program

TSI is excited to tutor students all over the world in various subjects. Please click on an intended subject below to register for the mailing list.

All proceeds from this initiative will be used to support education and empower students with better resources.

Click here for the class catalog, class information, and tutor contacts.

Mathematics

With our experienced math tutors, students will learn analytical skills and concepts, including geometry, number theory, and probability, to help them succeed in math, both in school and for various math competitions, such as MATHCOUNTS and AMC 8. Click to get started!

English

Students will develop a solid foundation in English grammar, vocabulary, reading, writing, speaking, and listening. The course places a strong emphasis on effective communication, enabling students to express themselves with confidence and clarity in various real-life scenarios. Click to get started!

Foreign Languages

Students will delve into the intricacies of languages such as Spanish, French, and more. The course offers a well-rounded curriculum that encompasses grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, and cultural context. Click to get started!

Physics

With our experienced physics tutors, students will gain a fundamental understanding of the laws governing the natural world, learning analytical, conceptual as well as intuitive skills in kinematics, dynamics, energy, and electricity, to prepare for high-school level algebra-based physics curriculum. Click to get started!

Volunteer

Apply to join our team of volunteer tutors, and build a strong connection with each one of us! A good way to earn your hours! Click to get started!

HSPT Prep

The course covers all sections of the exam, including verbal skills, quantitative skills, reading comprehension, mathematics, and language. Through a structured curriculum and targeted practice materials, students will develop a deep understanding of the test format, learn effective time management techniques, and strengthen their critical thinking and problem-solving abilities. Click to get started!
